Nasim — Lead Architect & Visual Designer

Nasim is a multidisciplinary creator with over a decade of experience bridging the gap between structural integrity and aesthetic soul. Throughout his career, he has collaborated with international studios to redefine luxury through a minimalist lens, specializing in high-end residential interiors and comprehensive brand identities.

His work is driven by the philosophy that every corner of a room and every pixel of a logo must serve a purpose. By mastering advanced tools like Revit and V-Ray, he transforms abstract concepts into tangible realities, ensuring that his clients don't just see a design, but experience a vision brought to life with surgical precision.
